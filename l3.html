Paris, France

Paris, the capital of France, is known for its art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. The Eiffel Tower is one of the most iconic landmarks in the world.

New York, USA

New York City is famous for its skyline, Central Park, Broadway, and the Statue of Liberty. It is a melting pot of cultures and a hub of international business.

Tokyo, Japan

Tokyo, Japan's bustling capital, mixes the ultramodern with the traditional, from neon-lit skyscrapers to historic temples. The Tokyo Tower is a must-see.
